Basic saving tips

  • Set air-conditioning to 24–25°C — every degree lower adds noticeably more energy use.
  • Unplug chargers and appliances on standby; idle "phantom load" still draws power.
  • Use natural daylight where possible instead of daytime lighting.
  • Wash clothes on a cold or quick cycle when the load allows it.
  • Swap old bulbs for LED — they use a fraction of the energy for the same brightness.
